# Verify

Home Assistant changes that needed verification (per the `ha-verify` skill / `CLAUDE.md`'s Process section) but couldn't be confirmed live at the time ‚Äî usually because the test is gated by a real-world condition (time of day, physical presence, a sensor event) that wasn't true when the change was made. Each entry records what to verify, the acceptance criteria, and why it was blocked, so it isn't silently forgotten.

Running `/verify` with no arguments works through the `Pending` entries below. Once confirmed, move the entry to `Verified` (with the date and outcome) rather than deleting it, so there's a record ‚Äî matching `ISSUES.md`'s convention of updating status in place.
